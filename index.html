LOTUS

The lotus flower is scientifically known as Nelumbo nucifera.

It is the national flower of India and Vietnam.

Lotus is an aquatic plant with large, beautiful flowers that symbolize purity, beauty, and spiritual awakening.

It grows in muddy water but blooms beautifully above the surface, representing strength and resilience.

LILY

The Lily flower is scientifically known as Genus Lilium.

Lilies are famous for their elegant shape, sweet fragrance, and vibrant colors.

They symbolize purity, devotion, renewal, and prosperity in many cultures.

Lilies are among the most popular ornamental flowers around the world.

ROSE

The Rose flower is scientifically known as Rosa rubiginosa.

Roses are admired for their beauty, fragrance, and romantic symbolism.

Different colors represent different emotions: Red for love, Yellow for friendship, White for purity, and Pink for admiration.

Roses are widely used in perfumes, decorations, gardens, and even food products.
